# Experience

This directory holds session-scoped reflection on what the work was like, not a
duplicate technical log of what changed.

## What belongs here

- what surprised you
- what shifted in understanding
- what felt harder or simpler than expected
- what emerged that was not obvious at the start

## What does not belong here

- load-bearing technical doctrine
- implementation instructions
- architecture decisions
- plan content

If durable technical guidance surfaces here, `consolidate-docs` audits it and
graduates it to the right permanent home.

## Lifecycle

- `session-handoff` is the capture edge for experience
- `consolidate-docs` is the audit edge

Experience files are allowed to stay subjective. The audit exists to protect
that purpose while rescuing any technical insight that should not remain here.
