# Roadmap

## Summary

The repo grows in three layers:

1. keep the agentic surface coherent and transferable
2. strengthen reusable runtime boundaries and audits
3. keep the demo application small while proving the template stays executable

## Near term

- preserve planning, memory, and adapter parity
- keep repo audit and hooks honest
- maintain a small but complete demo surface

## Ongoing constraint

This repo is a template foundation, not a feature product. Every roadmap move
must preserve that identity.
